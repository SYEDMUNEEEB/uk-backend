View and prove documents for obtaining a UK visa

Сheck your сertificates of sponsorship

If you were informed that your employer issued you a certificate of sponsorship, then you can check it by entering the CoS number of this document.

The Certificate of Sponsorship number it is essential information for work visa applications in the Worker and Temporary Worker. A certificate of sponsorship, also known as CoS, is issued by a sponsor who is licenced to hire migrant workers. These documents are essential for those who apply for work visas in the UK.

Enter the CoS number of your sponsorship certificate in the search box to check information about the availability of the document. After confirmation, enter your passport number for more information.
